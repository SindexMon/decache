FAQ

Q: How do I watch the videos I find?

The best program to play these videos is VLC Media Player. Once installed, you can select everything in the "Videos" folder and drag them in. If the list of videos disappears, CTRL+L will reopen it.

Q: I found a video, but it's in a weird format. Should I convert it to MP4?

DON'T do anything to the video before sending it over. It's very important that we receive the *raw* video file for verification.
