Internet stuff is important.

And... you might have some of it.

When you visit a website, all the files it has to load in (like images, videos, games, etc.) are temporarily stored in what's called the cache. After a few days of use, as new files come in, these files are deleted to save space.

Mostly.

Time and time again, it's been proven that there are too many glitches to say that for sure. Many people have a treasure trove of old, sought-after internet stuff on their old computers - and don't know it.

Decache will automatically find these files, scan them, and compare them against hundreds of pieces of lost media.

Why should I use it?

As more and more people check their computers, the odds of any of these bits of internet history being found skyrocket. The biggest video with a segment found using this strategy, Super Mario 64 big star secret, was found by someone who didn't even remember watching it.

Additionally, many of these videos have had cash bounties placed on them, most notably those by penguinz0, who has offered $50 USD for each of his 100+ deleted videos.
